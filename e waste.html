How the Process Works

1. Search: Find the nearest certified agent on the map.
2. Prepare: Back up data, erase devices, and separate batteries.
3. Drop or Pickup: Visit a drop point or schedule a pickup.
4. Recycle: Devices are recycled/refurbished following standards.
